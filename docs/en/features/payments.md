# Payments

Track and record payments for reservations.

## Features

### Payment Statistics
- Total payments count
- Pending payments count
- Total amount collected

### Payment List
- Table view of all payments
- Shows reservation, amount, method, status, and date
- Filter by payment status

### Record Payment
- Click "Record Payment" to log a new payment
- Select reservation
- Enter amount, payment method, and date
- Supports partial payments

### Payment Methods
- Cash
- PIX (Brazilian instant payment)
- Credit card
- Debit card

### Status Badges
- **Pending**: No payment recorded
- **Partial**: Partial payment made
- **Paid**: Full amount paid
